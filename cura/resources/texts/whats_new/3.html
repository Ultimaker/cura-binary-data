Default is now Balanced

Our Print Profile Experts have evaluated years of research resulting in the UltiMaker profiles and recalibrated them. With these insights the visual, engineering and draft profiles work better than before, and we now offer a default profile that strikes a better balance between the three. To reflect the perfect harmony between these three intents, we updated the default profile to “Balanced”. With these changes you can now choose from Visual, Engineering, Draft, or Balanced profiles for more materials in order to get great results that match your needs.
