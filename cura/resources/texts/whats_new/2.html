Quality improvements for S6/S8

We expanded the available print cores on the UltiMaker S6 and S8, so you can now also slice for AA 0.25, AA 0.4, AA 0.8, CC 0.4 and CC 0.6. New draft intent profiles for PLA, Tough PLA, ABS, and PETG have been added and significantly improved printed part quality for PLA, Tough PLA, ABS, and PETG.
