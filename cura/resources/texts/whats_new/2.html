UltiMaker PET CF profiles added

Intent profiles have been added for our newest material, UltiMaker PET CF. Giving users of UltiMaker S series printers access to professionally tailored print profiles for this high-performance composite. PET CF can also be annealed to increase the strength, stiffness, and thermal resistance of your part. To make this as easy as possible, PET CF has an annealing profile that will automatically scale your part to compensate for changes that occur during the annealing process!
